Added [AudioShow Crates](/audioshow/crates/) as a companion format to the episode archive.

The first shelves include [the first rule](/audioshow/crates/the-first-rule/), a 31-track path through late-90s big beat, trip-hop, sampledelia, and Fight Club pressure, and [A Pink Floyd Experience](/audioshow/crates/a-pink-floyd-experience/), a 71-track long-form map through Pink Floyd's psychedelic, pastoral, monumental, and post-war worlds.

Crates keep the AudioShow archive's curatorial style while giving longer moods and scenes their own room.
