Launched [SoundDrop](https://sounddrop.audiofool.blog/) and added it to [Projects](/projects/sounddrop/).

SoundDrop is a campus music map. You pick a place, upload an image, write a short memory or prompt, generate music through MERIC, then choose a version to publish or keep private.

The public-facing project page documents the product shape and links to the open-source frontend UI. The backend, deployment stack, and model service stay private.
