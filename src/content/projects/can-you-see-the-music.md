## Overview

**Can You See The Music** is a music data visualization project.

It studies music through direct and indirect attributes: lyrics, audio features, albums, artists, release time, sentiment, genre, playlists, and popularity. The project treats music as both sound and data, then asks what becomes visible when those layers are organized together.

## Project Shape

The work combines analysis, visualization, and a written report. Its questions are broad: how lyrics change over time, how audio features differ across styles, how sentiment appears in popular music, and how artist collaboration patterns develop.

## Why It Fits

This is an early version of a recurring Audiofool theme: music is not only something to hear. It is also something to map, model, classify, and visualize.
